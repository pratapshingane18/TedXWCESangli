About TED

TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is a global nonprofit organization that aims to spread ideas and knowledge through short, powerful talks. Established in 1984, TED brings together thought leaders, innovators, and experts from various fields to share their ideas and insights on a wide range of topics, including science, technology, culture, arts, and more.

TedX Community and Event Reach

  • 1.
    Global Impact
    Over 28,000 TEDx events held in 170+ countries, with 150,000+ talks delivered and over 1 billion views on YouTube, making a significant global impact in spreading ideas and inspiring change.
  • 2.
    Inclusivity and Diversity:
    TEDx events feature a diverse range of speakers, with 50% women speakers and over 40% speakers of color (as of 2021), promoting inclusivity and diversity in ideas and perspectives shared on the TEDx stage.
  • 3.
    Empowerment and Innovation:
    TEDx events are independently organized, with over 40,000 organizers worldwide, fostering innovation and empowerment in local communities, universities, and youth organizations, with thousands of events focused on social issues, technology, health, arts, and culture.
